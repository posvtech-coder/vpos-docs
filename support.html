⚡ How to Report an Issue

When contacting support, please include the following to help us resolve your issue faster:

  • Your account / business name
  • Branch name (if branch-specific)
  • Device ID (if device-related — visible in the Admin App under Devices)
  • Description of the issue — what happened and what you expected
  • Screenshot or screen recording if possible
  • When it started — approximate date and time

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

My billing device is not syncing. What should I do?

Check that the device has an active internet connection. Go to Admin App → Devices → select the device and tap "Force Sync". If the issue persists, contact support with the Device ID.

My subscription has expired. Will billing stop?

No. Your billing devices will continue to operate in offline mode. However, cloud sync and admin app access may be restricted. Contact us to renew your subscription.

How do I add a new manager?

Log in to the VPOS Admin App → go to your Branch → Managers → Add Manager. Fill in the manager's details and assign them to one or more branches.

How do I add a new branch?

In the Admin App → Branches → Add Branch. The maximum number of branches allowed is determined by your subscription plan.

A manager cannot log in. What do I do?

Verify the manager has been assigned to at least one branch in the Admin App. If the issue continues, contact support with the manager's email address and your business name.

Can I get a refund on my VPOS subscription?

VPOS subscriptions are non-refundable. Please refer to our Terms & Conditions for the full refund policy.

How do I register a new billing device?

Open the VPOS Admin App → Devices → Add Device. Use the camera to scan the QR code displayed on the billing device during its first boot. Contact support if the QR code does not appear.

I need more branches than my plan allows. What do I do?

Contact our support team to discuss an upgrade to your subscription plan.
