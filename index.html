Privacy Policy

VPOS Admin — by Value Tech Solutions

Internal Use Only  ·  Restricted Access

📋 Overview

VPOS Admin is a closed, invitation-only business management application. It is not available to the general public. Access is strictly limited to authorised personnel — including shopkeepers, branch managers, service agents, and administrators — who have been explicitly granted access by Value Tech Solutions or an authorised shopkeeper account.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the application collects, how it is used, and how it is protected. By using VPOS Admin you agree to the practices described here.

Effective Date: March 26, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 26, 2026

🗂️ Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the application for authorised business users:

Account & Identity

  • Full name, email address, mobile number
  • Profile photograph (optional, uploaded by the user)
  • Employee code and Aadhaar number (for internal identity verification only)
  • Business address and branch details

Business / Operational Data

  • Sales records, billing transactions, inventory data
  • Branch and device registration information
  • Customer hold-order and return records
  • GST and tax configuration settings

Device & Technical Data

  • Device model and Android version (for compatibility diagnostics)
  • Firebase installation ID and FCM push notification token
  • Crash reports and error logs (via Firebase Crashlytics)

🔐 App Permissions

The following device permissions are requested and the reason for each:

📷 Camera Scanning QR codes to register billing devices, and uploading profile photos.
📍 Location Verifying the physical location of branch devices during device registration.
🗄️ Storage / Files Importing product inventory via Excel files and exporting sales reports.
🔔 Notifications Receiving real-time alerts for sales activity, device status, and system events.

Permissions are requested at the time they are needed. You may deny any permission; however, the corresponding features will be unavailable.

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticating and managing your account securely
  • Providing role-based access (admin, shopkeeper, manager, service agent)
  • Displaying sales analytics, inventory, and business reports
  • Registering and managing point-of-sale billing devices
  • Sending push notifications about business events relevant to your role
  • Diagnosing technical issues through anonymised crash reports

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

☁️ Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Authentication), hosted in the asia-south1 (Mumbai) region. Google Firebase complies with ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, and GDPR standards.

Security measures in place include:

  • Firebase App Check — prevents unauthorised apps from accessing backend APIs
  • Role-based Firestore security rules — users can only access data relevant to their role
  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2+
  • Firebase Authentication with custom claims for verified role enforcement
  • Crash logs are anonymised and used only for debugging

🗑️ Data Retention

User account data is retained for as long as the account remains active. Upon account deactivation or deletion by an administrator, personal profile data is removed within 30 days. Business transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and accounting compliance.

🔗 Third-Party Services

The application integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase – Authentication, database, storage, crash reporting, notifications
  • Google Play Integrity – Device attestation for release builds

No third-party advertising or analytics SDKs are used.

👶 No Data from Minors

VPOS Admin is a strictly professional business tool intended for adults employed by or affiliated with authorised organisations. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 18.

✅ Your Rights

Authorised users may request the following at any time by contacting their administrator:

  • Access to personal data held about them
  • Correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion of their account and associated personal data
