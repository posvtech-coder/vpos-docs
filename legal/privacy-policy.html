📋 Overview

VPOS is a closed, business-only Point of Sale management system developed by Value Tech Solutions for supermarkets and retail stores. Access is strictly by invitation — all accounts are created by the Value Tech Solutions team. There is no public self-registration.

This Privacy Policy describes what information VPOS collects, how it is used, stored, and protected. By using any VPOS application (Admin, Billing, or Billing Offline), you agree to the practices described in this policy.

🗂️ Information We Collect

Shopkeeper / Business Account Information

Collected during onboarding by our team:

  • Full name, email address, mobile phone number
  • Business name and branch address(es)
  • Aadhaar ID number (optional — collected solely for identity verification purposes and stored securely)

Branch & Staff Information

  • Branch names, addresses, and configuration settings
  • Manager accounts: name, email, phone, assigned branches
  • Billing device identifiers and registration details

Inventory & Business Data

  • Product names, prices, categories, GST slabs, and images
  • Sales transactions, bill records, and payment details
  • Subscription plan details and device count

Customer Billing Data (Optional & Consent-Based)

Collection of end-customer information during billing is entirely optional. Shopkeepers may collect customer name and phone number only with the explicit consent of the customer. Value Tech Solutions does not mandate collection of customer data.

Device & Technical Data

  • Device model, Android version, and unique device identifier
  • Firebase installation ID and push notification token
  • App crash logs and error diagnostics

📷 App Permissions

The following device permissions may be requested:

📷 Camera Used by administrators and staff to register billing devices (QR scanning), and by shopkeepers and managers to add product images to inventory.
🖼️ Storage / Media Accessing device storage to upload product images and import/export inventory and report files.
🔔 Notifications Receiving real-time alerts for sales activity, device events, and subscription status.
🌐 Internet Syncing data with Firebase cloud services and communicating with VPOS backend systems.

You may deny any permission; however, the corresponding features will be unavailable.

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the VPOS platform
  • To manage shopkeeper accounts, branches, staff, and billing devices
  • To process and record sales transactions and generate reports
  • To send important system and subscription notifications
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve application performance
  • To verify business identity and comply with applicable regulations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

☁️ Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Storage), hosted on Google's secure cloud infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Access to data is controlled by Firebase Authentication with role-based custom security claims. Each shopkeeper can only access their own data. Firestore security rules enforce isolation between accounts.

Aadhaar ID numbers, where optionally provided, are stored in a restricted Firestore collection accessible only to authorised administrators.

🔄 Data Retention

  • Active account data is retained for as long as the account is active
  • Sales and billing records are retained for a minimum of 3 years to comply with business and tax record requirements
  • When an account is deactivated, data is retained for 90 days before permanent deletion unless legal obligations require longer retention

👶 Children's Privacy

VPOS is a business application intended solely for use by adults operating commercial businesses. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.
