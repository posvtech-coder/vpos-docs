🏢 About VPOS

VPOS (Value Point of Sale) is a cloud-based point-of-sale management system designed for supermarkets and retail businesses. The platform enables shopkeepers to manage billing devices, branches, staff (managers), inventory, and sales transactions from a centralised interface.
